Smart Timer

Mobile App

About the project

My Role:
  • UI/UX design
  • Prototyping
  • Interactive Design
  • Brand Identity

Smart Timer is equipmentless time measurement system. Allows overview of all measurements from different sports. Measurement history and benchmark, goal setting.

Mobile app will use as measurement gate and does not require the use of special equipment. It allows you to track and compare performance, analyze the result and adjust the goals for coaches and teams. Designed for teams and individuals.

Brainstorming the rough concept of the app and architecture

The primary goals of our research were:
Understand sports' psychological and social aspects — what about sports that make people so engaged with it?
Look in the market for references and best practices regarding news/sports apps and other UX and UI trends that could apply to this project
Understand how the editorial design evolved and what were the aspects about it that we could and should apply to the new app.

Brand Identity

Before jumping into the high-fidelity prototype I needed to develop the brand for the product because this is a completely brand new app.

The good part about developing the brand along with the style guide is that we can always think of branding in terms of user experience, for example when choosing the colors, the name, the labels of the categories, all that can be transformed when we have the user experience and interface point of view. And that’s how the Smart timer app was born!

UI Kit & Design system

During development, I took great care to follow the design system. All interface elements have been entered into a style guide, and their behaviour has been described for developers.

Also I using what we call "atomic design". It made my design process much easier and more cohesive.

Professional App Version

That measurement system in everybody’s pocket will change the way how young athletes improve their perfomance. The coach can measure whenever needed with minimum effort and use phones from player to setup any exercise, also can track the performance and adjust goals for the team and individuals.

Basic Version

The players get their result from events and try the exercise at home, compete with friends and improve performance.

Smart timer makes the sport an online game.

For everyone. Everywhere. Any time.

Ready to test!

I then needed to test this new product. To do so, I had to update all wireframes and build a perfect hi-fidelity prototype :)
